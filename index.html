Crux – Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 5, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Crux (the “App”) handles information when you use it. The App is developed and provided by Brandon Duarte (“we”, “us”, or “our”).

1. Overview

Crux is a climbing route documentation app. It allows you to create and manage climbs, attach photos, add notes, tags, grades, and visual annotations. The App is designed to work primarily on your device. We do not run our own servers for the App, do not require an account, and do not use third-party analytics SDKs.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Create in the App

All content you create or import into the App is stored locally on your device. This may include:

  • Climb details (titles, grades, ratings, timestamps)
  • Photos you choose from your library or take with your camera
  • Annotations on photos (hold markers, beta sequences, and related metadata)
  • Tags, notes, and other text you enter
  • Send logs and status information you choose to record

2.2 Device and OS-Level Data

We do not intentionally collect device identifiers, location data, or other personal information. However, Apple may provide us with anonymized and aggregated information (for example, crash logs or basic usage statistics) through App Store Connect and related developer tools. This information does not identify you personally and is used only to improve app stability and performance.

3. How Your Information Is Stored

All app data is stored locally on your device using Apple’s system storage mechanisms (for example, SwiftData). We do not run our own backend or database for this App.

Because storage is local:

  • Your data is tied to your device and your iOS backup settings.
  • If you uninstall the App, iOS will delete the App’s local data.
  • If you enable iCloud backups, Apple may back up the App’s data as part of your device backup.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the operation of the App, including:

  • Displaying your climbs, photos, tags, notes, and annotations
  • Allowing you to search, filter, and organize your climbs
  • Improving app reliability using anonymized crash and performance data provided by Apple

We do not use your information for targeted advertising, user profiling, or cross-app tracking.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or otherwise share your personal data with third parties. Your data may be shared only when:

Apple and other platform providers (such as iCloud, if enabled) may process your data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6. Data Security

We rely on Apple’s operating system and security features to protect the data stored on your device. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and your Apple ID, including enabling features such as device passcodes, Face ID/Touch ID, and iCloud security options.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only as long as it remains on your device:

  • You can delete individual climbs, notes, and photos from within the App.
  • You can delete all App data by uninstalling the App from your device.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

9. Your Rights

Because we do not operate our own user accounts or remote servers for this App, most control over your data is exercised directly on your device (for example, by editing or deleting climbs, or uninstalling the App). If you have questions about how your data is handled, you may contact us using the information below.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App or applicable laws. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
